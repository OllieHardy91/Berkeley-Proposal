02 — Standards

Five standards built into every See3D delivery.

Minimum deliverable standards apply to every project, without exception and without an extra invoice line. The level of polish a premium buyer expects, baked in as the default — not an upgrade.

01
Clean reflections
Mirrored doors, polished marble, glass facades and bath panels — capture sequence is planned around them so See3D stays out of the picture. The buyer sees the unit, not the equipment that captured it.
02
Tidy nadirs and zeniths
Floor and ceiling pole points are cleaned in post-production. Coffered ceilings and detailed flooring read as designed — not as platform artefacts.
03
Site-staged before capture
Doors positioned, blinds set, signage removed, surfaces prepped — the same care a stylist brings to a show suite shoot, applied to every space.
04
Natural navigation paths
Scan points placed for buyer progression — not operator convenience. The viewer walks the property the way an agent would walk a client through it, with sightlines that flow.
05
Premium hardware
Realsee Galois M2 — 134 MP per scan point, ±20 mm LiDAR at 10 m. Specifically chosen for the level of detail premium buyers expect when they zoom into a finish, a fitting, or a view.
Quality control is a deliverable standard, not an upsell. Every tour, every capture, every time.
